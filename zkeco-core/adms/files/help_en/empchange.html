Personnel adjustment is daily maintenance of existing personnel, primarily including: personnel transfer (adjust department, title change, employment type change, adjust area), personnel departure, and delete personnel.

Personnel transfer:


Includes adjust department, title change, employment type change, adjust area. Operation steps are as follows:
(1) Click [Personnel] - [Transfer] - [Add] to show the personnel transfer information edit interface;

(2) Select personnel and enter transfer information. After selecting one or more items from transfer fields (multiple choice allowed): department, title, employment type, area, it will show: before transfer (automatically selected), after transfer (selected from the pull-down menu);
(3) After editing, click [OK] to save and quit.
Operation functions of personnel transfer can also be fulfilled in the [Personnel] - [Personnel] menu. Now we take adjust department for example:
Click [Personnel] - [Personnel], and select the person subject to department adjustment from the personnel list, click [Adjust Department] button, select the department to be transferred to, and click [OK].
